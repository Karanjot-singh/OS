The gcc compiler indergoes through 4 steps to convert the file into an executable:

1. pre-processing  - Comments are removed, header files and Macros are expanded

	//command: cpp hello.c > hello.i
	gcc -E hello.c
	
2.Compilation
	//command: gcc -S hello.i
	gcc -S hello.c

3. Assembly step
	//command: as -o hello.o hello.s
	gcc -c HelloWorld.c -o HelloWorld.o

4. Linking: GCC does dynamic linking
	//command: ld -o hello.exe hello.o ...libraries...
	gcc -o hello hello.c
